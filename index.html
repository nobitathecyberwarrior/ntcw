> SYSTEM_INITIALIZATION

Founded on February 23, 2024, by Md Yousuf Ali, Nobita The Cyber Warrior (NTCW) — also known as NT Warrior — is an ideological and dedicated cybersecurity agency. We operate as a trusted defensive shield in the digital realm, committed to keeping the cyberspace safe, clean, and secure.

> CORE_MISSIONS

  • National Cyber Defense: Securing digital assets, monitoring threats, and protecting the country's cyberspace from malicious attacks.
  • Anti-Blasphemy Operations: Building strong resistance against defamatory content and tracking individuals who spread religious hatred online.
  • Eliminating Obscenity: Enforcing a zero-tolerance policy against the distribution of explicit and immoral content across social media and the web.

> ACTIVE_OPERATIONS

  • Vulnerability Scanning & Continuous Threat Monitoring.
  • Advanced OSINT (Open-Source Intelligence) & Information Gathering.
  • Cyber Counter-Operations to dismantle malicious digital networks.

> OUR_VISION

NTCW is more than a traditional cybersecurity team; we are an elite group of ethical digital warriors. By combining advanced technical expertise with strong moral values, we constantly fight to serve as a leading cyber defense force for the nation.
